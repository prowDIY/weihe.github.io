Short Bio

Wei He is currently a professor at Wuhan University. Between 2018.01 and 2021.11, he was a researcher at Geoinformatics Unit, the RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP), Japan.
His research interests include remote sensing image restoration, computer vision, low-rank modeling, and deep learning.
